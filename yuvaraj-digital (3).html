Common questions

Frequently asked

How much does it cost to work with Yuvaraj Digital?+
Pricing depends on the service and scope. Monthly retainers start from ₹8,000/month for basic social media management and go up based on ad spend managed, number of platforms, and complexity. I offer a free audit and strategy call first — no payment until you're comfortable with the plan.
How quickly will I see results?+
Paid ads (Google & Meta) typically show initial results within the first 2–4 weeks as we gather data and optimise. SEO is a longer game — meaningful organic traffic improvements usually show within 3–6 months. I'll always set realistic expectations upfront, never promise overnight miracles.
Do you work with businesses outside Chennai?+
Absolutely. While I'm based in Chennai, I work with clients across India — Coimbatore, Madurai, Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, and even internationally. All communication happens over WhatsApp, email, or Zoom, and I'm responsive across time zones.
Will I have to sign a long-term contract?+
No lock-in contracts. I work on monthly retainers — you can pause or stop with 30 days' notice. I believe in earning your trust every month through results, not trapping you with paperwork. Most of my clients have been with me for 1–3 years because the results speak for themselves.
What budget do I need for Google or Meta Ads?+
For meaningful results on Google or Meta Ads, I recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000–₹20,000/month. My management fee is separate and scales with your spend. Below this threshold, the data is too thin to optimise effectively and I won't promise results I can't deliver.
How do you report results? Will I understand it?+
Every month I send a clear report in plain Tamil or English — no confusing dashboards, no unexplained metrics. The report covers: what we spent, what we earned, what worked, what didn't, and the exact plan for next month. If you want a detailed call to walk through it, that's always available too.
Can I reach you on WhatsApp during campaigns?+
Yes — and this is one of the biggest advantages of working with me directly. You can WhatsApp me at +91 7200603280 and I personally respond, usually within a few hours. No raising tickets, no waiting for a "dedicated account manager" to relay your message to someone else.
